For other uses, see Liverpool F.C. (disambiguation).Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. The club competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. Founded in 1892, the club joined the Football League the folowing year and has played its home games at Anfield since its formation
